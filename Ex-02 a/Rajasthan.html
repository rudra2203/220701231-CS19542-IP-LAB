Rajasthan

  • Rajasthan is home to many magnificent forts and palaces, such as the Amer Fort, Mehrangarh Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal.

  • A significant portion of Rajasthan is covered by the Thar Desert, also known as the Great Indian Desert.

  • The capital of Rajasthan is Jaipur. Known as the "Pink City" due to the distinct color of its buildings

  • Rajasthan is renowned for its handicrafts, including block-printed textiles, blue pottery, leather goods, and jewelry. The state's artisans produce unique and high-quality crafts that are popular worldwide.

  • Rajasthan's history is deeply intertwined with the Rajput kings and their valorous tales. The state has numerous historical sites and monuments that reflect its glorious past.
