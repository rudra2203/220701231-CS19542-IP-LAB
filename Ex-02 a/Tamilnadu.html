Tamilnadu

  • Tamil Nadu is renowned for its rich cultural heritage and historical significance. The state is home to many ancient temples such as the Brihadeeswarar Temple in Thanjavur, Meenakshi Temple in Madurai.

  • Tamil Nadu is a hub for classical Indian arts, especially Bharatanatyam, one of the oldest classical dance forms, and Carnatic music, a major genre of Indian classical music.

  • Tamil Nadu has a unique political history characterized by the Dravidian movement, which emphasizes social justice, rationalism, and the upliftment of the lower castes.

  • Chennai, the state capital, is often referred to as the "Detroit of Asia" due to its thriving automobile industry.
